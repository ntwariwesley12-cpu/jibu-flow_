Common Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours.

Can I schedule a delivery?

Yes! You can specify your preferred delivery time when placing an order. We offer flexible scheduling to meet your needs.

Do you deliver outside Kigali?

We primarily serve the Kigali metropolitan area with same-day delivery. Provincial deliveries are available with 1-2 business day service.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive tracking information via SMS and email. You can also call us for real-time updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, mobile money (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money), and bank transfers for your convenience.

What should I do if I have a complaint?

Your satisfaction is our priority. Please contact our support team via phone or email, and we will address your concerns promptly.

Can I set up a recurring order?

Yes! We offer subscription plans for regular deliveries. Contact our sales team to set up a recurring order that fits your needs.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we provide special pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales department for more details and a custom quote.

How do I update my contact information?

You can update your contact details by reaching out to our customer service team via phone or email.

What safety measures are in place for deliveries?

Our delivery personnel follow strict health and safety protocols, including wearing masks and sanitizing hands to ensure safe delivery.

Where can I find your office?

Our main office is located in Kigali, Rwanda. You can visit us during business hours or schedule an appointment by calling our customer service line.

who are the founders of Jibu Flow Water?

Jibu Flow Water was founded by KABOOKYA Milly and KANYONI AIME, who are dedicated to providing clean and safe drinking water to communities in Rwanda.
