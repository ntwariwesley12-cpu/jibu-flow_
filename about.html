About Jibu Flow

Founded by KABOOKYA Milly

Jibu Flow is Rwanda's premier bottled water company, committed to providing the purest, most refreshing drinking water to our customers. Founded in 2026, we've been serving communities across Rwanda with our advanced purification technology and eco-friendly practices.

Our mission is to make clean, healthy water accessible to everyone while protecting our environment through sustainable packaging and responsible water sourcing.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Founded by visionary entrepreneur KABOOKYA Milly, Jibu Flow began with a simple yet powerful idea: every Rwandan deserves access to clean, safe drinking water. What started as a small operation in Kigali has grown into Rwanda's most trusted water delivery service.

Commitment to Quality

We use state-of-the-art purification technology that removes impurities while preserving essential minerals. Every bottle undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets the highest standards of purity and safety.

Environmental Responsibility

Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. Our bottles are 100% recyclable, and we actively participate in community clean-up initiatives across Rwanda.
