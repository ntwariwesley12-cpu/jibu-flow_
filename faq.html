Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and delivery

Is Jibu Flow water safe to drink?

Yes! Our water undergoes rigorous testing and multiple purification processes to ensure it's 100% safe and pure. Every bottle is lab-certified and meets Rwanda's highest water quality standards.

How long does delivery take?

Same-day delivery in Kigali and surrounding areas for orders placed before 2 PM. Rural delivery may take 1-2 business days depending on location.

Do you offer subscriptions?

Yes! We offer flexible monthly and yearly subscription plans with up to 20% discount and free delivery. You can cancel or modify your subscription anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, bank transfers, and Momo Pay for secure and convenient payments. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Can I return empty bottles?

Yes, we encourage bottle returns for recycling. Get RWF 100 credit per returned bottle. This helps us reduce plastic waste and keep costs affordable.

Is your packaging eco-friendly?

Absolutely! All our bottles are 100% recyclable and made from BPA-free plastic. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact.

What if my water arrives damaged?

If you receive damaged or leaking bottles, contact us immediately. We'll arrange a replacement delivery at no extra cost within 24 hours.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can update your delivery address anytime through our website, mobile app, or by calling customer service. Changes take effect on the next delivery.

Do you deliver on weekends?

We offer weekend delivery in Kigali metro area. Please specify your preferred delivery time when placing your order. Additional fees may apply for weekend delivery.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive SMS and email updates with tracking information. You can also call our customer service for real-time updates.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order is 1 bottle for one-time purchases. For subscriptions, the minimum is 4 bottles per month to qualify for subscription discounts.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The price you see is the price you pay. Delivery is free on orders over RWF 50,000 or with active subscriptions. Any additional fees are clearly disclosed.
